No duplication waiver is active for dataset `pilot_medium_v1 1.3.0`. Its offline
exact and character n-gram checks pass. Real BGE-M3 validation also passes: 101 of

dataset is Phase 6 eligible without a waiver. Real-site manuals still require a
separate review.
